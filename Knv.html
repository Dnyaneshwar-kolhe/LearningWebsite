    K.Narkhede Vidyalaya and Kanishtha Mahavidyalaya

    We have science, arts and commerce faculty for the Kanishta Mahavidyalaya. The Vidyalaya is Govt. recognized and grantable. In the science section we have limited seats. We cannot accommodate all the students passing 10th S.S.C. We started computer education in 1995, probably being the first in the District. The Vidyalaya consists of trained and well educated teachers as per Govt. requirement. For the commerce faculty, along with teaching theory, we also invite Chartered Accountants to guide our students and bank Managers to educate our students to teach the practical working followed in banks. We are conducting tally classes for commerce students.
